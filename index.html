Projects

The project involved a comprehensive evaluation and redesign of the Save the Koalas website, a charity organization dedicated to rehabilitating koalas. The objective was to enhance user experience and establish credibility by creating a more user-friendly and visually appealing website. The redesign focused on streamlining the donation process to ensure a seamless and trustworthy experience for users, ultimately supporting the organization's important cause.

The project centered around designing and developing a comprehensive travel app that enables users to create personalized itineraries, collaborate on organizing tasks, access destination information, and effortlessly book flights and accommodations. By adopting a user-centered approach, the app aimed to revolutionize the travel planning experience. The focus was on creating an intuitive and convenient all-in-one solution, enhancing the overall journey for travelers and their companions. The app aimed to simplify the complexities of travel planning while providing a seamless and enjoyable user experience.

The project involved redesigning the Australian government website Workforce to improve user experience and ease of use. By applying strategic design decisions and a user-centered approach, the goal was to create a more intuitive and accessible platform for workforce-related information. The project encompassed redesigning the home page and site map, streamlining navigation, enhancing visual aesthetics, and optimizing the user journey to establish a trustworthy and efficient digital resource.
